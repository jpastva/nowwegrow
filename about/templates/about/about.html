Welcome to NowWeGrow!

NowWeGrow is a service designed to connect farmers and merchants with their local communities. Our platform is designed to allow local farmers to tell their stories and showcase their quality products to customers in search of healthy choices that align with their needs. Merchant memberships are reasonably priced, and provide access to an attractive e-commerce platform and customizable profiles to reach more customers with minimal overhead.
